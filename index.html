Hankyo - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Hankyo

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Hankyo respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the application.

2. Data Collection & Storage

We collect your email and username for account creation via Supabase. Your "Hankyos" (tiles), notes, and canvas positions are stored securely on our cloud servers. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

All financial transactions are processed by Apple or Google via RevenueCat. PeterfromChile does not have access to your credit card information.

4. Account Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time via the app settings. This action is permanent and wipes all data from our servers.
